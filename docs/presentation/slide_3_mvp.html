The MVP Focuses on Three Core Capabilities

The system combines AI sales automation, CRM pipeline management, and logistics orchestration into a single dashboard. This is not a full platform build — it is a focused MVP that addresses Ali's three biggest bottlenecks.
AI Sales Engine
Vapi AI Voice Agent
Automated outbound calling with real-time transcript streaming and keyword extraction.
CRM + Pipeline
Supabase (PostgreSQL + PostGIS)
Lead tracking, drip campaigns, credit checking, and human escalation workflows.
Logistics
Google Maps Routing API
Territory-based route grouping with truck capacity management and stop optimization.
