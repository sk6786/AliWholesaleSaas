AI Calls Follow a Four-Phase Workflow with Real-Time Validation

Every outbound call progresses through four visible phases in the dashboard. The AI agent performs real-time validation against Supabase during the call — checking inventory availability and credit limits before confirming any order.

2. Streaming

4. Summary
